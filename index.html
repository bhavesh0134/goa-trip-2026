💡 12 Money-Saving Pro Tips

1
Travel Sleeper class — saves ₹600–₹900 vs 3AC, perfectly comfortable for budget travel
2
Rent a scooter instead of autos/cabs — most economical way to explore Goa
3
Eat at local dhabas, not beach shacks — same food, 40–50% cheaper
4
Stay in hostel dorms — meet fellow travelers, share scooter costs
5
Carry train snacks from Nagpur — Nagpur oranges, local chivda, saves ₹200+
6
Shop at Mapusa/Anjuna Market — avoid beach-front shops (expensive)
7
Free beaches are the best beaches — Vagator, Agonda, Arambol, Butterfly Beach all free
8
Book train tickets ASAP — June is peak/tatkal season. Book 30–45 days ahead.
9
Avoid taxis from station — take local bus or pre-booked local autos
10
Carry a refillable bottle — save money and the environment
11
Carry sufficient cash from Nagpur — many small shops/beaches are cash only
12
June = Monsoon discounts! — 30–40% cheaper than peak season. Lush green Goa is magical.
⚠️ June Travel Notes

🌧️ Heavy rainfall — rain jacket mandatory
🌊 Rough seas — always check beach flags and lifeguard advisories
🤿 Water sports — many operators close in June. Plan sightseeing-heavy itinerary.
💚 Lush Goa — waterfalls, green hills, fewer crowds = magical experience
🦟 Health — carry mosquito repellent + stomach meds. Drink bottled/filtered water only.
